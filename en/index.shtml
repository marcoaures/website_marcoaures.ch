About me

Growing up in a craftsman family in Bavaria, I also started my career in the trades as a technical draftsman. My interest in creative and analytical topics, however, soon led me to study Media Studies and American Cultural Studies at FAU Erlangen–Nuremberg. At the same time, I gained my first experiences into the world of digital marketing as a working student at Siemens Healthineers in the User Experience and Content Management departments.

Love eventually brought me to Switzerland, where I continued my career as an content manager at OMH AG. In that role I helped build an open innovation platform for the Berner Kantonalbank. Today I still work part-time at BEKB, and, since 2024, I also offer my services independently as a freelance digital marketer.

I'm based in Olten, Switzerland, which is located in the middle between Bern, Basel, Zurich and even Lucerne. Most of the work happens remotely anyway, but when it makes sense I'm happy to come visit you on-site.

Shaped by my roots in the trades and growing up with a family business, I still place great value on tailored solutions, transparency, and communication at eye level. Thanks to my varied experience – from technical know-how and an affinity for new innovative methods to a feel for storytelling and data analysis – I develop unique solutions tailored to the specific needs of my clients.
